How to upload a resume

To upload a resume, first you need to add it to your USAJOBS profile.

Please read the below instructions before clicking Upload resume.

  1. Click Continue at the bottom of the pop-up message — we'll open a new tab in your browser and send you to the resume section of your profile in USAJOBS.
  2. Click Upload or build resume — you'll get another pop-up window. Click Upload resume.
  3. Choose the resume you want to upload and change the name if needed.
  4. Click Complete upload — you'll see the new resume in the resume portion of the documents section of your USAJOBS profile.
  5. Go back to the browser tab that says Open Opportunities.
  6. Go to the Resume section and click Refresh resume — you will see the new resume you uploaded.
  7. Select the resume you want to include in your application.
  8. Click Save and continue to finish your application.
